Description of the app

Lock In is an iOS application that helps users reduce screen time and digital distractions by blocking access to selected apps on their device. The App uses Apple's Screen Time API and NFC technology to enable and unlock app-blocking sessions.

Core features include:

  • Blocking selected apps on your device using Apple's Screen Time framework
  • Registering an NFC card or tag as a physical key to unlock app-blocking sessions
  • Configuring unlock timers — how long apps are accessible after an NFC unlock (1–15 minutes) and the cooldown period before you can unlock again (5–15 minutes)
  • Scroll to Sleep mode — a 4-hour unlock window intended for use before bed, with a 20-hour cooldown to prevent misuse
  • Emergency unlocks — a limited safety valve of up to 3 emergency unlocks per calendar month
  • Subscription customisation — Premium subscribers can adjust unlock and cooldown durations; free users have fixed defaults (5-minute unlock, 10-minute cooldown)
  • Optional subscription for access to full features
